Xaxis AI Marketplace Case Study

Xaxis provides media buying AI technology for B2B clients. In 2021, Xaxis submitted a project proposal to WPP for a marketplace. The marketplace is an e-commerce B2B business network and exchange of digital solutions.

Project Background

Summary Details

  • My Role: UX Researcher and recruited participants
  • Research Type: Remote
  • Company: Xaxis
  • Company Type: B2B Corporate Media
  • Industry: Artificial Intelligence, Media Buying

  • Director of Product

üí£ The Problem

Both Sides

Business Problem: Xaxis lost money and revenue because they were not able to deliver custom solutions requested by their clients when it was in demand.

User Problem: User requests for custom algorithm solutions were not being met because Xaxis didn't have the technical capability in the existing web platform, Copilot. This led to unhappy clients.

üí° Xaxis Project Proposal

To attempt to resolve the business and user problems mentioned, Xaxis pitched a project proposal for a digial marketplace concept to the parent company, WPP.

The marketplace was to enable Xaxis to create custom algorithm metric solutions for clients by including self-maker tools for their data scientists to have the technology to create them.

WPP approved the project proposal and gave Xaxis üíµ $8 million to fund the project in 2021!

Project Process

My Approach

Research Planning Stage

üéØ Research Objectives
  • To determine whether the Marketplace is a solution for a seller (maker) to upload their algorithms and to network to find buyers.
  • To determine whether the Marketplace provides more selections for the buyer to select algorithms and to request custom algorithms. Right now, users are limited to whatever Copilot provides in the system with three DSPs.
ü§î Some Research Questions
  • For Sellers: What is your workflow process for creating custom algorithms?
  • For Sellers: What kind of metric KPIs or algorithm solutions do most clients request? (most in demand)
  • For Buyers: How often are you able to decide which strategy to select on your own?
  • For Buyers: What are your most preferred campaign outcomes? (conversions, clicks, views, etc) Why is that?
